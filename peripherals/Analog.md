Analog IO
========

* KEYWORDS: Analog,ADC,DAC,A2D,D2A,Built-In

Espruino contains both Analog Inputs ([[ADC]]) and Analog Outputs ([[DAC]]).

You can also create 'fake' Analog Outputs with [[PWM]], you can also record and play back [[Waveform]]s.
